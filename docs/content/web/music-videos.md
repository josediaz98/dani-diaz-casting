# Music Videos

## Section Intro

In music videos, every face shapes the visual world. I've worked on 30+ projects across different genres, creating castings that elevate the energy and aesthetic of each piece. Credits include artists such as **Alex Ubago & Seye, Nia Correia & Guaynaa, La Blackie, Cora Novoa,** among others.

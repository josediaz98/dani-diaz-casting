## Bio

**+100 CASTINGS COMPLETED**

I am a Peruvian Casting Director and creative professional with more than 12 years of experience leading projects across Latin America and Europe. My background in advertising and brand storytelling gave me a solid foundation to transition into Casting Direction, where I have now led over 100 processes for film, TV, and advertising. I specialize in discovering authentic talent and creating inclusive, human-centered casting methodologies that connect stories with diverse voices.

**My work builds bridges between cultures and markets, with recent projects spanning from Spain to Colombia and Los Angeles.**

## Extended Texts

### Manifesto / About
I'm an international casting director working across Europe and Latin America, focused on finding authentic talent with real presence and emotional truth. I believe casting is not just about faces—it's about discovering people who carry stories within them.

I approach every project with a human-centered vision, creating safe and collaborative processes where talent can show who they truly are.

### Work Philosophy
My work bridges cultures, languages, and identities. From street casting to curated searches, I look beyond the obvious to find characters that feel alive, honest, and connected to the world we live in.

Diversity is not a box to tick—it's a natural reflection of reality.

### What I Offer
I lead casting processes from brief to final selection for film, advertising, and music videos, ensuring a seamless workflow and strong creative alignment with directors and producers.

Every face is chosen with intention. Every decision serves the story.

### Unique Value
With experience in over 100 casting projects across Europe and Latin America, I specialize in discovering new talent, building strong ensembles, and elevating narratives through authentic representation.

I don't just cast roles—I shape worlds.

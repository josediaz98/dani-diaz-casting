Campaigns that connect feel real. I cast for advertising projects across **Europe and Latin America**, finding talent with presence, attitude, and on-camera credibility. I've worked with brands like **Decathlon, Montana Colors, KOI Vodka, El Lobo, Oxford Generation**, and more—always prioritizing diversity and the perfect fit for each brand's identity.
